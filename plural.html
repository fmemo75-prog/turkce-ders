Turkish Plurals & Vowel Harmony

1. Vowel Groups

Turkish vowels are split into two groups. The last vowel of a word tells you which suffix to use.

Group Vowels Suffix
Back (Thick) a, ı, o, u -lar
Front (Thin) e, i, ö, ü -ler

2. The Rule

Last vowel is Back → Use -lar
Last vowel is Front → Use -ler

💡 The Exception (Loanwords):
Some words from Arabic/French use -ler even if they look "back".
Example: Saat (Hour) → Saatler (Clocks)
