Locative Case (Bulunma Hâli)

The suffixes for "in, at, on" are -da, -de, -ta, -te

Last Vowel: a, ı, o, u➡️ -daOdada (In the room)
Last Vowel: e, i, ö, ü➡️ -deEvde (In the house)

⚠️ Hard Consonants (Fıstıkçı Şahap):

F S T K Ç Ş H P

If the word ends with these letters, D changes to T:

-taMutfakta (In the kitchen)
-teMarkette (At the market)
